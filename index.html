About the College

A focused institute, built around one discipline.

Shri Ram Chandra Paramedical College is dedicated entirely to building skilled Medical Laboratory Technologists for hospitals, diagnostic centres, and pathology labs across Madhya Pradesh and beyond. Our laboratories, faculty, and curriculum exist for one purpose: turning students into technicians that doctors and patients can rely on.

Located behind Devanta Hospital Campus on Singhpur Road, the campus gives students proximity to a working hospital environment from day one — bridging classroom theory with the rhythm of real diagnostic work.

Programs

Two paths into Medical Lab Technology.

Choose the program that matches your stage and goals. Both are taught by the same lab-first philosophy — different depth, different duration.

PROGRAM / DMLT

Diploma in Medical Lab Technology

Duration
2 Years
Eligibility
10+2 (Science) or equivalent
Seats/Intake
Limited — apply early
Mode
Full-time, on-campus

A practical, fast-track diploma covering hematology, biochemistry, microbiology, and pathology lab techniques — ideal for students who want to enter the diagnostics workforce quickly.

PROGRAM / BMLT

Bachelor in Medical Lab Technology

Duration
3 Years
Eligibility
10+2 (Science) with required percentage
Seats/Intake
Limited — apply early
Mode
Full-time, on-campus

An in-depth degree building advanced diagnostic, analytical, and lab-management skills — suited for students aiming for senior technologist or supervisory roles.
